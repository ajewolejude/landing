Who We Are ?

Grabbs Integrated Services Ltd (GRABBS) is a Transport and Logistics company operating out of Lagos, Nigeria with a focus on the local and regional supply chain market.

Incorporated in 2013 to carry on business as a support service company, Grabbs has grown to be a major player in the heavy duty logistics segment associated with outbound port services as well as FMCGs like cement and petroleum products.

Grabbs currently operate sizable fleet of flatbed trucks and covered body trucks and has developed a fleet growth plan to acquire hundreds of trucks to consolidate on her market share.
